🔒

End-to-End Encrypted

MLS (RFC 9420) group encryption with forward secrecy. AES-256-GCM with key commitment tags. The server stores only ciphertext.

👤

No Phone Number Required

Sign up with a username and password. Or use passkeys, OAuth, or SSO. True anonymity by default.

🎤

E2EE Voice and Video

SFrame (RFC 9605) encrypted WebRTC. Voice, video, and screen share never leave your device unencrypted.

🤖

AI Agents

Add AI assistants to encrypted channels. Multi-provider: Anthropic, OpenAI, Ollama local. Agents hold their own keys.

🔑

Passkeys and OAuth

FIDO2 WebAuthn with YubiKey and Touch ID. OAuth 2.0 with Google, GitHub, Discord, Apple. TOTP 2FA.

🌐

Self-Hostable

Three commands to deploy. Docker Compose with automatic TLS via Caddy. Runs on a $5 VPS.
