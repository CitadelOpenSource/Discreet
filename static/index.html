What you get

🔐

E2EE Messaging

MLS (RFC 9420) for groups, Signal Protocol for DMs. The server stores ciphertext — it cannot decrypt your messages, even under compulsion.

🎙️

Voice & Video

WebRTC peer-to-peer calls with SFrame (RFC 9605) encryption. Screen sharing. The media server routes encrypted frames it cannot read.

🤖

AI Agents

LLM-powered bots that hold actual MLS leaf secrets and participate in group key exchange. Multi-provider: Anthropic, OpenAI, Ollama. Patent pending.

🏠

Self-Hosting

Single Rust binary. Docker Compose, Raspberry Pi, or Oracle Cloud free tier. Your data never leaves hardware you control. $5/year electricity on a Pi.

📡

Proximity Mesh

Encrypted BLE text and Wi-Fi Direct voice without internet. Messages queue offline and sync on reconnect. Designed for environments where connectivity fails.

🛡️

AutoMod & Safety

Profanity filtering, spam detection, invite blocking, mention limits. Argon2id passwords, TOTP 2FA, one-time recovery keys, per-connection rate limits.
